Linestyles:

'-': Solid line
'--': Dashed line
':': Dotted line
'-.': Dash-dot line

Colors:
Named colors: 'red', 'blue', 'green', etc.
Hexadecimal colors: '#FF5733', '#4287f5', etc.
RGB tuple: (0.2, 0.4, 0.6)
RGBA tuple: (0.1, 0.2, 0.3, 0.5)

Colormaps:
'viridis': Perceptually uniform colormap (default in Matplotlib)
'plasma': Colormap with high contrast and vibrant colors
'coolwarm': Colormap with both cool and warm colors
'cividis': Colormap designed for those with color vision deficiencies

Markers:
'o': Circle marker
's': Square marker
'D': Diamond marker
'^': Triangle marker
'+': Plus marker
'x': Cross marker
